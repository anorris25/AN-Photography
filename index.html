My name is Anne Norris and I am a portrait photographer.

I bought my first DSLR camera in 2016, and I consider it one of the best purchases of my life. I took it to every school dance, field trip, sporting event, family vacation, you name it. I started scheduling photoshoots with people and learning how to properly edit photos. It became more of a passion than a hobby at that point. I will always love seeing people adore themselves in photos I've taken of them.
